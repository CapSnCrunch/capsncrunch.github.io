About Leafcast

Leafcast is for book lovers who want to listen to the books they already own. Turn your physical books into personal audiobooks, whether they're rare, out-of-print, or simply not available on other platforms.

We believe your bookshelf deserves a voice.

Why Leafcast?

Upload As You Go: No need to scan an entire book at once — add pages over time! Most books take minutes to upload and give you hours of listening.

No Subscriptions: Pay only for what you use. No monthly fees. No locked shelves.

Preserve What Matters: Keep your favorite paperbacks, journals, or heirloom texts alive in a new format.

Free Classic Library: Explore a curated collection of public domain works — available to listen anytime, for free.

Human-Like AI Voices: Clear, expressive narration designed for long-form listening.

Save Time, Keep Ownership: Revisit personal notes or niche finds on the go — without sacrificing your own collection.

Whether you're listening to an obscure nonfiction title or preserving a fragile family memoir, Leafcast helps you take your library with you — without giving it up.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert my physical books to audiobooks?
Simply photograph or scan pages from your book using the Leafcast app. Our AI will convert the text to high-quality audio narration. You can upload pages individually over time — no need to scan entire books at once.
What types of books work best with Leafcast?
Leafcast works with any printed text — from rare first editions and out-of-print books to personal journals, academic texts, and family heirlooms. Even handwritten notes can be converted to audio.
How much does it cost?
There are no subscription fees or monthly charges. You only pay for the pages you convert to audio. We also offer a free library of public domain classics you can listen to anytime.
How long does it take to process my book pages?
Most individual pages are processed within minutes. The exact time depends on text density and image quality, but you'll typically have your audio ready to listen very quickly.
What about copyright and my personal content?
Your personal books and the audiobooks you create from them remain entirely yours. We don't share or distribute your content. Leafcast is designed for personal use of books you own.
