Pareidolic Painting

(2 / 19 / 22)

You've likely experience Pareidolia; perceiving patterns in random data. I've recently started making art embracing this false pattern recognition by making 'found doodle' art in paintings on cardboard. My process has been to mix paints, spread them around a piece of cardboard in little blobs, and sketch in all of the weird faces and animals I notice after it's dried. Adding a little bit of organization by laying out the paints following some sort of color gradient gives a great overall look at the end and an experience I would highly encourage trying!
