Layolay

<  2 / 24 / 23  >




In my last year of college, I built the startup Layolay with my roommate, Christian Boswell, who came up with the idea for a platform which allows 3D modelers to upload, manage, and sell their models to customers without managing any of the actual inventory, shipping, or 3D printing.

The hundreds of hours I invested into making this site were a major motivating force in my switch from pursuing graduate school to software development. I wrote more about the process of creating this website in my first Software Snapshot if you're interested in reading about it.
