Robo Rosewater

(12 / 26 / 21)

A friend of mine recently showed me the twitter account @RoborosewaterM, an account which posts Magic the Gathering cards generated by a recurrent neural network. As an avid player of the game, I found these to be a lot of fun, but figured it would be nice to view the cards on something other than twitter.

I decided to build a database to view the cards and sort through them by a few basic search parameters. I've also been learning React this break so I figured this would be a good opportunity to try out.
