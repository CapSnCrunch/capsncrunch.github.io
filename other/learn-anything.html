I recently entered the Google AI Hackathon as a fun way of experimenting with generative AI APIs. My submission was learnanything.io, which is a site for generating fully structured courses based on any topic, especially those that aren't accessible elsewhere online.

This was the largest web project I have undertaken entirely on my own and in the course of a month I was able to set up the CI/CD and deployments, serverless functions, and all of the nice little UI/UX things that I've taken note of now that I've spent a couple years in the professional software engineering world. I have a full article with some of the more interesting implementation details here.
