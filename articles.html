Every few weeks, I try to write a short article on an interesting topic. These topics range from math, to computer science, to linguistics, or whatever I'm attracted to at the moment. My goal with writing these articles is to motivate myself to learn and understand new things often and to hopefully pass some of my excitement about them on to my readers. If you're wanting to know more about a topic or have something cool to share, please feel free to contact me!
